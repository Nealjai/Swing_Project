How We Classify the Market

The scanner’s first job is to determine the overall market health. We do this by comparing SPY’s close with its 200-day simple moving average.

SPY Price
→
Compare with 200-Day Average
→
If SPY > SMA200 → Bull Regime
If SPY ≤ SMA200 → Weak Regime

One Regime, One Engine

Based on regime, the scanner activates exactly one engine designed for that environment.

Market Regime
→
Bull Regime → Activate Bull Engine
Weak Regime → Activate Weak Engine
